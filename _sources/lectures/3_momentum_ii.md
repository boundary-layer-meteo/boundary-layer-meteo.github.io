The starting point is the equation as derived in the previous section.

$$
  \pd{\ubar}{t}
=
- \pd{\overline{ u^\prime w^\prime }}{z}
+ f \left( \vbar - v_g \right) \\
  \pd{\vbar}{t}
=
- \pd{\overline{ v^\prime w^\prime }}{z}
- f \left( \ubar - u_g \right)
$$
